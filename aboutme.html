Toxic Harvest

Solo project with original gameplay scripts created over a 2 month period
Scripted game mechanics using object-oriented-programming in C#
About: Survival farming game in a radioactive environment

Crafting Mechanic

- Created an original script for a crafting mechanic
- Catch fish in the lake and turn them into their corresponding fertilizers
- Plants will become hungry and require certain types of fertilizers

Farming Mechanic

Original scripts created for the following features:
- Plant growth stages and progression
- Plants requiring fertilizers at different growth stages
- Plants glow and damage you if they are not fed after 1 min in their radius
- Harvesting has a chance to drop radioactive goo that works towards the
goal of developing a cure to your radioactive sickness
